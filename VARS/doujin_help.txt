```
Doujin Request Format: 

{1}doujin [operand] [Stuff] (more operands) (more stuff) .....

The Operands:  "i", "e", "a" and "c" 	can have multible objects! (parse by " ")

Operands:


{0}r (only r, without anything)
	Returns a complete random Doujin

{0}i 	or {0}it or {0}itag or {0}itags or {0}i_t or {0}i_tag or {0}i_tags 
	Include a one or multible tags to the search
		[No Autocomplete, don't make typos]

{0}e 	or {0}et or {0}etag or {0}etags or {0}e_t or {0}e_tag or {0}e_tags 
	Exclude a one or multible tags from the search
		[No Autocomplete, don't make typos]

{0}a	or {0}anime or {0}animes
	Search by one or multible animes
		[Try to Autocomplete: nisekoi --> Nisekoi / ????]
		
{0}c	or {0}char or {0}characters or {0}character
	Search by one or multible characters  
		[Try to Autocomplete: golden_darkness --> Golden Darkness / Konjiki no Yami]

{0}s	or {0}stars or {0}star
	The Minimum Star Rate (0-5) 
		[Numbers only]

{0}pmax	or {0}p_max or {0}page_maximum or {0}page_max
	The Maximum Site Count 
		[Numbers only]

{0}pmin	or {0}p_min or {0}page_minimum or {0}page_min
	The Minimum Site Count 
		[Numbers only]

{0}=
	Rawsearch
		[Without _, just use normal Spaces]


e.g. the command:
{1}doujin {0}i full_color harem {0}e dirty_old_man {0}c golden_darkness {0}a To_love-ru {0}p_min 15 {0}s 4

Trys to Return a Doujin with:

Include Tags: Full Color, Harem
Exclude Tags: Dirty Old Man
Characters: Golden Darkness / Konjiki no Yami
Anime: To Love-Ru / To LOVE ?
Pages: 15+
Stars: 4+
```
