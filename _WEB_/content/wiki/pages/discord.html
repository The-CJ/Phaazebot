PhaazeDiscord

Intro

Discord is the 'main' platform of Phaaze.
The Phaaze project startet as a Discord Bot, If you want to know more, look at the History.

Be sure that you have the needed permissions to run a commands,
check out the PhaazeDiscord Permission-Rules for more.

How to add it

To add Phaaze to your server, you need to have a role with the 'Manage Server' or 'Administrator' permission,
or even better be the server owner.

Just go to phaaze.net/discord/invite and call Phaaze to a server.
